Privacy Policy

Nebula Flap by Polyakov Games
Effective date: May 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Nebula Flap handles information when you play the game.

1. Information We Collect

Nebula Flap does not require you to create an account and does not ask you to provide your name, address, phone number, precise location, or other direct personal details.

The game may store gameplay progress locally on your device, such as high score, coins, selected skins, unlocked content, sound settings, and gameplay settings.

2. Local Game Data

Progress data is stored on your device so that the game can remember your score, coins, skins, and settings. This data is used only to provide gameplay functionality inside Nebula Flap.

If you uninstall the game or clear the app data, this local progress may be deleted.

3. In-App Purchases

Nebula Flap may offer optional in-app purchases, such as ad-free access, PRO features, or cosmetic content. Payments are processed by Google Play. We do not receive your full payment card details.

Google Play may process purchase-related information according to Google's own privacy policies and terms.

4. Third-Party Services

The game may use Google Play services for distribution, purchases, licensing, crash reporting, or platform functionality. These services may process technical information according to their own policies.

5. Children's Privacy

Nebula Flap is a casual game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We do not share your direct personal information with advertisers or data brokers.

7. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect the functionality of the game. However, no digital system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, the effective date above will be updated.
