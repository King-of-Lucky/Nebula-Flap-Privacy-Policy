# Nebula Flap Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** May 28, 2026  
**Game:** Nebula Flap  
**Developer:** Polyakov Games  
**Contact:** bogdan.poliakoff@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how Nebula Flap handles information when you play the game.

## 1. Information We Collect

Nebula Flap does not require you to create an account and does not ask you to provide your name, address, phone number, precise location, or other direct personal details.

The game may store gameplay progress locally on your device, such as:

- high score;
- collected coins;
- selected skins;
- unlocked skins and content;
- sound settings;
- gameplay settings, including flight settings where available.

This information is used only to provide and improve the gameplay experience inside Nebula Flap.

## 2. Local Game Data

Nebula Flap stores progress data locally on your device so that the game can remember your score, coins, skins, and settings.

If you uninstall the game or clear the app data, this local progress may be deleted.

## 3. In-App Purchases

Nebula Flap may offer optional in-app purchases, such as ad-free access, PRO features, subscriptions, bonus content, or cosmetic items.

Payments are processed by Google Play. Polyakov Games does not receive your full payment card details. Google Play may process purchase-related information according to Google's own privacy policies and terms.

## 4. Advertising

Nebula Flap may include advertisements in the free version of the game. If ads are shown, third-party advertising services may process limited technical information in order to display and measure ads.

If an ad-free or PRO version is purchased, advertisements may be removed according to the purchase terms shown in the game and/or on Google Play.

## 5. Third-Party Services

Nebula Flap may use third-party services provided by Google and/or Unity, including but not limited to:

- Google Play services;
- Google Play Billing;
- Google Play Console tools;
- crash reporting or diagnostic services;
- advertising services, if ads are enabled in the game.

These services may collect and process technical information according to their own privacy policies and terms.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Nebula Flap is a casual game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at bogdan.poliakoff@gmail.com and we will take appropriate action.

## 7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

We do not share direct personal information with advertisers or data brokers. Some third-party services used by the game, such as Google Play or advertising services, may process technical data according to their own policies.

## 8. Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect the functionality of the game and the integrity of local gameplay data. However, no digital system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## 9. Data Retention

Local gameplay data remains on your device until you uninstall the game, clear app data, or reset progress if such functionality is available.

Purchase history and payment-related data may be retained by Google Play according to Google's policies.

## 10. Your Choices

You may be able to manage or delete local game data by clearing the app data or uninstalling the game from your device.

You can manage Google Play purchase and subscription settings through your Google Play account.

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, the effective date above will be updated.
